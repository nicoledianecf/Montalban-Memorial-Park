Lots

The park consists of family plots, lawn lots, apartment-style tombs, and a columbarium.

Family Lot (currently unavailable)
A family lot, or mausoleum, where multiple family members
are laid to rest, often in an above-ground structure. It provides
a private, secure space for the family's remains and serves as
a permanent memorial, offering room for future generations
and personal touches like engravings.

Columbary (available)
The Columbarium has eight (8) levels. Each level tells
stories of lives lived to the fullest. These sacred spaces
can hold up to four urns or two precious bone remains in
one unit.

Lawn Lot (available)
This type of burial plot is often chosen for its simple,
serene appearance, allowing for a well-maintained and
uniform lawn aesthetic. Lawn Lots are designed to accommodate
either single or multiple burials within a family and
provide a peaceful, open environment for visitors to
reflect and remember their loved ones.

Apartment (currently unavailable)
The Apartment has eight (4) levels. It has vertical burial
spaces where multiple graves are stacked in a shared structure.
Each compartment holds one or more remains, offering a practical
solution in areas with limited space.
